Title: Machine Learning Applications in Healthcare: A Comprehensive Review

Abstract: This paper presents a comprehensive review of machine learning applications in healthcare. 
We examine various ML techniques including deep learning, natural language processing, and computer vision 
as applied to medical diagnosis, drug discovery, and personalized treatment. Our analysis covers 
over 500 recent publications and identifies key trends, challenges, and opportunities in the field.
