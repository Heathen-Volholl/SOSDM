Title: Artificial Intelligence in Climate Change Research: Current Applications and Future Prospects

Abstract: Climate change represents one of the most pressing challenges of our time. This review 
examines how artificial intelligence techniques are being applied to climate research, including 
climate modeling, extreme weather prediction, and environmental monitoring. We analyze recent 
developments and discuss future research directions.
